My Experience with Reactive Programming [Part -1]

My Experience with Reactive Programming

In this blog post, I'll be sharing my learnings on building microservices using concepts of Reactive Programming.
My last project for a business use case where a service was expected to act as a core capability for other services to improve user experience. Context :
Current business has different product services with their respective user journeys. There are some steps in those user journeys which can be developed as a core capability as they are replicated across different products and partner services.
When working towards providng seamless user experience to end-customers and for other partners, the business faces following hurdles:
1. Architecture design makes it difficult to intergrate with new partners and products while catering to exising users.
2. Repetitive steps across user journeys makes it difficult to effectively update / enhance those steps for uniform user experience across products.
Core requirement :
1. Improve onboarding for new partners & products' services by having an easily configurable setup.
2. Extract common steps and create microservice whihc is developed as a core capability for products to enhanced as required and same gets reflected across different services.
It can be in future developed for partners to consume directly in their products services.
